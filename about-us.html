About Us - The Library Locator Team

Our Mission:

In our effort to combat social inequality we are striving to provide easier access to literature for children. The “Little Library” system has often partnered with local communities to provide smaller and more convenient library locations but lacks the ability for finding informal book donation spots. Therefore, our website focuses on a more grassroots approach, allowing people to register their own book drop-off locations, fostering deeper community connections, and increasing local literacy.

Who We Are:

We are three students of Rice University participating in the 2020 HackRiceX competition motivated by a love for code and desire for social change.
